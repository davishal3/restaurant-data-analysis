# Restaurant Data Analysis

This project presents an end-to-end analysis of a restaurant dataset using Python. The main objective was to explore the data, identify useful business insights, and present the findings in a clear and structured format through visualizations and a final PDF report.

The analysis covers important areas such as top cuisines, city-wise restaurant distribution, price range patterns, online delivery trends, rating behavior, cuisine combinations, geographic distribution, and restaurant chains.

---

## Project Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze restaurant data and extract meaningful insights that can help understand customer preferences, restaurant trends, pricing patterns, and business performance across different locations.

---

## Tasks Covered

The project includes the following analysis tasks:

1. **Top Cuisines**
   - Identified the top three most common cuisines
   - Calculated the percentage of restaurants serving these cuisines

2. **City Analysis**
   - Found the city with the highest number of restaurants
   - Calculated the average rating for restaurants in each city
   - Identified the city with the highest average rating

3. **Price Range Distribution**
   - Visualized the distribution of restaurant price ranges
   - Calculated the percentage of restaurants in each price range category

4. **Online Delivery**
   - Determined the percentage of restaurants offering online delivery
   - Compared average ratings of restaurants with and without online delivery

5. **Restaurant Ratings**
   - Analyzed the distribution of aggregate ratings
   - Identified the most common rating range
   - Calculated the average number of votes received by restaurants

6. **Cuisine Combination**
   - Found the most common cuisine combinations
   - Examined whether some cuisine combinations tend to receive higher ratings

7. **Geographic Analysis**
   - Plotted restaurant locations using latitude and longitude
   - Observed restaurant clustering and location patterns

8. **Restaurant Chains**
   - Identified restaurant chains present in the dataset
   - Analyzed their ratings, outlet count, and popularity
